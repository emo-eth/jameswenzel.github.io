Me & Your Girl

In February 2016 I started a song-a-week series called Me & Your Girl, which is an emo-R&B EP about unrequited love. The title is a tongue-in-cheek reference to the casual misogyny of hip-hop and R&B. I wrote, sang, performed, recorded, engineered, etc. each song myself.
